# /competitive-news

Research recent news and developments about your competitors.

## Instructions

**First time running this skill:** Use AskUserQuestions to ask:
1. "Who are your top 3-5 competitors?"
2. "What topics or areas do you want to track? (e.g., new features, pricing changes, funding, partnerships, leadership changes)"
3. Save the answers to a `competitive-context.md` file so you remember next time.

**Subsequent runs:** Read `competitive-context.md` for the competitor list and topics.

**Then:**
1. Search the web for recent news about each competitor (last 2 weeks)
2. For each competitor, summarize:
   - What happened (1-2 sentences)
   - Why it matters for us (1 sentence)
   - Source link
3. End with: "Anything here worth discussing?" and flag the 1-2 most strategically relevant items

## Output Format

### [Competitor Name]
**[Headline]** — [1-2 sentence summary]
*Why it matters:* [1 sentence on strategic implications]
[Source link]

---

*Most relevant this week:* [1-2 items flagged for discussion]

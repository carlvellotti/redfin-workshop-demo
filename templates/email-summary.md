# Executive Email Template

**Format:** Concise, decision-oriented. Written for a busy executive who will spend 60 seconds on this.

---

**Subject:** [Initiative Name] — Recommendation for Review

Hi [Name],

**Bottom line:** [One sentence — what we recommend and why.]

**The problem:** [2-3 sentences on the user problem and why it matters now.]

**What we found:** [3-4 bullet points of key evidence — research + data.]

**Our recommendation:** [2-3 sentences on what we think we should build, the scope, and the expected impact.]

**What we need from you:** [Clear ask — approval, feedback, a meeting, etc.]

Happy to walk through the details whenever works for you.

[Your name]
